squarespace, wix, format and cargo keep your work locked inside their database. gesso is just a normal repository of plain files on GitHub. every page, every artwork, every design choice sits in a file you can open, copy, and carry anywhere.

  • every artwork, a plain text file. titles, descriptions and order live in readable markdown. open one in any editor and you can read it.
  • your images, untouched. the originals sit in src/assets exactly as you uploaded them. download the folder and you have every file.
  • all your design, one file. colors, fonts and layout are a single settings.json. nothing is trapped in a database only we can read.

move hosts anytime. if gesso vanished tomorrow, your live site keeps running. that is what owning your portfolio actually means.

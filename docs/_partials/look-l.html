the handoff

we set it up, hand you the keys, and step out.

gesso runs the one-time setup with GitHub and Netlify, then gives you everything. no monthly rent, no platform that owns your work. squarespace, wix, format and cargo keep your site locked inside their walls for $16 to $49 a month. yours is just plain files you already own.

your keys
  1. 01

    it's your github repo

    every page and image is a plain file in a repository under your own name. open it, copy it, keep a backup. no database only we can read.

  2. 02

    move hosts whenever

    an optional domain runs about $12 a year and points wherever you like. take your files to any host the day you feel like it. nothing to unlock.

  3. 03

    live even if we're gone

    your site doesn't depend on gesso to stay up. if we vanished tomorrow your portfolio keeps running, exactly as it is.
